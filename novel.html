The Three-Body Problem (Brief Intro)

About

The Three-Body Problem is a hard science fiction trilogy by Liu Cixin. It tells the story of humanity's first contact with an alien civilization called Trisolaris, and the struggles of survival in the vast, dark universe.

Main Themes

The novels explore cosmic survival, the "Dark Forest" theory, human ethics, and the rise and fall of civilizations at astronomical scales.

Key Ships (Illustrative)

Light-speed Ship: A near-light-speed craft, allowing interstellar migration but with severe limits.

Curvature Ship: Using curvature drive to bend space-time, enabling true faster-than-light travel.

Black Domain Ship: A survival plan that traps a region of space in slowed physics, protecting but isolating a civilization.
