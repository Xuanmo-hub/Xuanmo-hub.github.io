What is Astronautics

Astronautics (or cosmology) is the practice of sending spacecraft out of the Earth's atmosphere and into outer space. Space flight is one of its main applications, and space science is its core field.As in aerospace engineering, limitations on mass, temperature, and external forces require that space applications withstand extreme conditions: the high vacuum, the radiation bombardment of interplanetary space, and the magnetic field of low Earth orbit. Space launch vehicles must withstand enormous external forces, and satellites experience large temperature changes in a very short period of time. [5] The extreme constraints on mass force force aerospace engineers to continuously save mass in their designs to maximize the actual payload that can reach orbit.
