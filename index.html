About the content

This website aims to share people's fantasies and ideas about interstellar travel in novels, movies and games. Even if they seem difficult to achieve, they are the embodiment of human exploration and creativity. In different types of art, some methods of interstellar travel are realistic, some are incredible, and some have scientific basis.Currently, this website will introduce the interstellar navigation methods in the movie Interstellar, the novel The Three-Body Problem, and the game StarCraft. Note: All of them are science fiction. Even if there is a scientific basis, they are still not real. The principle introduction is just a science fiction worldview.
