Stalkers utilize Phase Warp Technology, built into their armor, to alter their spatial coordinates.This technology is similar to the Protoss' Warp Gate, but operates on a smaller scale, enabling it to be used at the individual unit level.Due to its high energy consumption, Stalkers are limited to short, intermittent warps, unlike the long-distance warps of motherships or warp gates.

They maintain a connection to the "psychic network" on their warships or home planets through energy fields projected from pylons.The barracks itself isn't a "manufacturer" in the traditional sense, but rather a phase conversion device responsible for projecting units forged in the rear to the front lines.Units are initially "pre-constructed" in the phase energy plane, not physically formed.In other words, units are "assembled" in the void or energy state.When the barracks switches to "warp gate mode," it utilizes energy fields (such as the coverage area of a pylon to project these energy-state units to a specific location on the battlefield. At the target location, the units undergo phase stabilization, gradually condensing from energy into matter.
